Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a trip?

Tap the + button on the main screen, enter your trip name, dates, and location. You can add players and courses after creating the trip.

How do I invite friends to my trip?

Go to the Trip tab and find your Share Code. Share this code with your friends — they can join by entering it when they open the app.

How does iCloud sync work?

If iCloud is enabled on your device, trip data syncs automatically across your Apple devices. All players in the same trip will see live updates as scores are entered.

What are Side Games?

Side Games are popular golf formats like Skins, Nassau, Wolf, and Snake that you can run alongside your regular round. Set point values and the app tracks everything automatically.

What are Challenges?

Challenges let you create custom competitions based on any tracked stat (e.g. Most Birdies, Fewest Putts). Set a commitment like "Bragging Rights" or "Loser buys dinner" and the app tracks standings and results.

How do I see who owes what?

Go to the Side Games tab and select the Results section. It shows a complete summary of all game and challenge outcomes, net balances, and the minimum number of settlements needed.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes. All scoring and trip data is stored locally on your device. Weather and Spotify features require an internet connection, but core scoring works offline.

How do I report a bug?

Email us at ultimategolftripapp@gmail.com with a description of the issue, your device model, and iOS version. Screenshots are helpful too.

Troubleshooting

Scores aren't syncing between devices

Make sure iCloud is enabled in Settings > [your name] > iCloud. Both devices need to be signed into the same Apple ID. Try pulling down to refresh on the War Room screen.

The app is running slowly

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, make sure you're running the latest version of iOS and the app.

Weather isn't loading

Weather requires an internet connection and a course to be added to your trip. Check your connection and make sure at least one course is set up.

Spotify playlist won't connect

Make sure the Spotify app is installed on your device. Tap "Connect Spotify" in the War Room and follow the authorization prompts. If it fails, try logging out and back into Spotify first.

Privacy Policy

Ultimate Golf Trip App respects your privacy. Here's what you should know:

  • Data stored locally: All trip, scoring, and player data is stored on your device using local storage.
  • Optional cloud sync: If enabled, data syncs via Apple's iCloud (CloudKit) and is governed by Apple's privacy policy.
  • Spotify: If you connect Spotify, authentication is handled through Spotify's official OAuth flow. We do not store your Spotify password.
  • Weather: Your course location is used to fetch weather forecasts. Location data is not stored or shared.
  • No tracking: We do not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or sell any user data.
  • No account required: You can use the app without creating an account.
